# Glossary

**Application Service** — Operational view of an application or application stack. (Ch 7)

**Business Application** — Logical application used for design and portfolio planning. (Ch 5)

**Business Capability** — Stable description of what a business can do. (Ch 5)

**Business Service** — Service delivering value to business consumers. (Ch 8)

**Business Service Offering** — Specific consumable variation of a business service. (Ch 8)

**CSDM** — ServiceNow's prescriptive shared set of service-related definitions and modeling guidance. (Ch 1)

**CSDM Domain** — Group of model entities supporting related lifecycle activity. (Ch 2)

**CI Relationship** — Typed directional connection between configuration items. (Ch 9)

**Common Data** — Shared organizational, location, team, knowledge, and reference data. (Ch 3)

**Digital Value Network** — Connected view spanning strategy through delivery and consumption. (Ch 1)

**Dynamic CI Group** — Query-driven grouping of configuration items. (Ch 7)

**Foundation Domain** — Shared entities and lifecycle capabilities used across CSDM. (Ch 3)

**Goal** — Desired strategic outcome. (Ch 4)

**Information Object** — Meaningful information used or produced by business activity. (Ch 5)

**Planning Item** — Work considered or selected for planning. (Ch 4)

**Portfolio** — Governed collection of related investments or items. (Ch 8)

**Prescriptive Relationship** — Standard relationship type and direction recommended by CSDM. (Ch 9)

**Product Idea** — Proposed opportunity or improvement. (Ch 4)

**Product Model** — Definition of a product, separate from its instances. (Ch 3)

**Reference Field** — Direct structured link stored on a record. (Ch 9)

**Request Catalog** — Consumer-facing request channel linked to services or offerings. (Ch 8)

**SDLC Component** — Version-controlled component managed through software delivery. (Ch 6)

**Service Instance** — Specific operational occurrence of a service. (Ch 7)

**Service Portfolio** — Governed collection focused on services. (Ch 8)

**Strategic Priority** — Area receiving organizational focus. (Ch 4)

**Target** — Measurable result supporting a goal. (Ch 4)

**Technology Management Service** — Service used to manage technology. (Ch 7)

**Value Stream** — End-to-end sequence delivering value. (Ch 3)
